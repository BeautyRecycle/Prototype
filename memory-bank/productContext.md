# Product Context

## Problem Statement
Beauty product packaging often ends up in landfills. Consumers lack incentives and easy ways to track their recycling habits for these specific items.

## Solution
An app that gamifies the recycling process.

## Key Features
- **Dashboard:** Overview of user impact (Plastic Saved, Energy Generated).
- **Scanner:** Interface to scan packages (mocked or real implementation TBD).
- **Rewards:** Marketplace to redeem earned points.
- **Activity Log:** History of scanned items.

## User Experience
- **Gamified:** Points, rewards, and impact stats (CO2, kWh).
- **Visual:** Eco-friendly design with animations and stats.
